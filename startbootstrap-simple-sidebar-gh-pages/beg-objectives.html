    • Class #2

    Intro to Surveys

    1. Understand the purpose of learning evaluations (to evaluate progress toward the learning outcomes of missionary training and Preach My Gospel from “Introduction of Preach My Gospel” on page vii.)
    2. Take the entrance questionnaire and doctrinal foundation pre-assessment.

    The Authority and Power of Your Calling

    1. Understand that a missionary calling confers authority.
    2. Know that they can receive spiritual power as they receive priesthood ordinances and keep the associated covenants.
    3. Review the covenants they have personally made.

    Training Expectations #1

    1. Learn the importance of seeking the guidance of the Holy Ghost to help them as they prepare.
    2. Understand expectations for their missionary training.
    3. Know how to use their time in and outside of class.
    4. Become familiar with the class calendar and reoccurring events.

    Class #5

    Resto- Gospel Principles

    1. Render an account of their ability to “commit to memory the sequence of the teachings in each lesson” (Preach My Gospel, p. 26).
    2. Practice sharing principles in one of the lessons in Preach My Gospel, chapter 3 in simple, clear, and brief ways, in their own words, as guided by the Spirit.

    Resto- Deepen Your Doctrinal Foundation

    1. Practice “study[ing] the scriptures and treasur[ing] up the doctrine in each lesson” to help them fulfill their purpose, deepen their understanding and testimony, and share lessons that are simple, clear, and brief (Preach My Gospel, chapter 3).

    Resto- Prepare to Teach

    1. This lesson will help missionaries prepare to teach by making a lesson plan based on a specific person’s needs.

    Using Teachnology Righterously Part 1

    1. Use technology wisely and righteously to fulfill their missionary purpose
    2. Learn how to apply the safeguards for using technology to use technology appropriately

    Class #7

    Adjusting to Missionary Life Part 1

    1. Understand that stress is a normal part of life and is part of how we grow.
    2. Understand that too much stress for too long can lead to problems.
    3. Identify symptoms of stress using the self-assessment record.
    4. Learn strategies and suggestions from Adjusting to Missionary Life to help them (1) balance stress and (2) learn to rely on Heavenly Father and the Savior.

    Extend Invitations, Promise Blessings, and Bear Testimony

    1. Extending invitations.
    2. Helping people make and keep commitments.

    Invite and Help People Attend Church

    1. Plan to invite someone to attend church and help them have a meaningful experience.

    Class #8

    Preparing names to take to the temple

    1. Understand that helping people make and keep covenants on both sides of the veil is gathering Israel.
    2. Know how to find and print names using FamilySearch and Ordinances Ready.
    3. Know how to use “Learn About” (in a browser) or “View Person” (on the mobile app) for the person they perform temple ordinances for.
