Missionary Training Curriculum (Beginner)

  • Home Page

    • Class 1.2

    Greetings and Farewells

    1. I can exchange culturally appropriate greetings and farewells.
    2. I can recognize names and titles.
    3. I can identify different gestures and expressions when people meet and greet each other.
    4. I can greet others appropriately (teacher, district, friends, family, members). (e.g. correct usage of 'you').
    5. I can listen to people greeting each other and identify if they know each other, are friends, or are meeting for the first time. entrance questionnaire and doctrinal foundation pre-assessment.

    Class #1.3

    Family

    1. Identify my and others' family members.
    2. Give a detailed explanation of my family (age, occupation, number of siblings, etc.).
    3. List my family members' characteristics.

    Class #1.4

    The Godhead

    1. List the members of the Godhead and list their characteristics.
    2. Identify characteristics and titles of the Godhead.

    Class #1.7

    The Missionary Purpose

    1. Use technology to create a short video.
    2. State who I am and what I am doing.
    3. Share my video with others.
    4. Identify the 5 main things through which the gospel of Jesus Christ is received.
    5. Pronounce the letter sounds properly.
    6. Say the Lord's name with clarity.

    Class #2.1.

    Share a Simple Testimony

    1. Identify common phrases in a testimony.
    2. Identify simple truths about Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ.
    3. Share a sincere testimony about Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ.
    4. List attributes of God that I know to be true.

    Class #2.3.

    Personalize a Prayer

    1. I can give a personal prayer, a companionship prayer, and a prayer for others that contains few personal thoughts.
    2. I can identify common needs of others.
    3. I can ask for blessings for specific people or groups of people.
    4. I can ask questions to understand needs of others.
    5. I can identify things people are grateful for.
    6. I can express gratitude on behalf of others and myself.

    Class #2.4.

    Share a Scripture

    1. I can give a scripture reference.
    2. I can state a page number.
    3. I can pronounce the words clearly.
    4. I can ask someone to read a verse.
    5. I can thank them for reading.
    6. I can ask someone a simple question about what they read.
    7. I can invite someone to read the Book of Mormon.

    Class #2.5.

    Share Statements of Truth

    1. I can offer a simple testimony. [relying on memorized chunks]
    2. I can share a sincere testimony about Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ.
    3. I can state simple truths about God and Jesus Christ.
    4. I can understand and express how the basic teachings of Jesus Christ help me find happiness.
    5. I can identify familiar gospel terms in an inspirational message.

    Class #2.8.

    Talk about Food

    1. I can identify my favorite food.
    2. I can ask others what foods they like.
    3. I can communicate food allergies I have.
    4. I can make a shopping list of food for preparation day.
    5. I can list the food I like and don't like.
    6. I can demonstrate culturally appropriate table manners.
    7. I can enter a home in a culturally appropriate manner.

    Class #3.3.

    State Simple Truths

    1. Share a scripture about having faith in Jesus Christ.
    2. Ask basic questions to help someone think about a scripture they just read.
    3. Ask what a gospel term may mean to someone.
    4. Understand someone's thoughts about faith.
    5. Understand and identify the characteristics of faith.
    6. Ask someone to restate what I have taught.
    7. Ask and answer simple and common questions about Jesus Christ.
    8. Ask someone what I taught means to them.
    9. Share a sincere testimony about Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ, and faith.

    Class #3.5.

    Discuss My Schedule- Coordination

    1. Understand and respond to questions about availability in my schedule.
    2. Ask others about their availability.
    3. Compare schedules with other missionaries or those we teach.
    4. Schedule an appointment.
    5. Confirm with my companion the time, place and reason for a meeting.
    6. Express obligations/prior commitments.

    Class #3.6.

    Overview of Someone's Beliefs

    1. State what someone believes.
    2. Understand common expressions of belief from others.
    3. Understand when others describe how they show faith in God.
    4. Ask and answer questions about showing faith in Jesus Christ.
    5. Share someone's questions or concerns about God, church, and family.

    Class #3.7.

    Talk About My Environment

    1. Say my phone number and address.
    2. Ask others for their phone number and address.
    3. Fill out a form by recognizing the terms used therein.
    4. Hear a name spelled and accurately write down what I hear.

    Class #3.10.

    Share a Simple Testimony- My Conviction

    1. Offer a simple sincere testimony.
    2. State simple truths about what I believe.
    3. Express how the basic teachings of Jesus Christ help me find happiness.
    4. Identify my beliefs in the scriptures and Preach My Gospel.
    5. Share a simple personal experience that supports my testimony.

    Class #4.3.

    Basic Social Contexts

    1. Coordinate a member present lesson.
    2. Introduce myself via phone call and text.
    3. Set an appointment via phone call and text.

    Class #4.5.

    Explain a Scripture

    1. I can introduce the topic of a scripture.
    2. I can give background information about a scripture.
    3. I can explain the scripture in my own words.
    4. I can make simple connections between scriptures and those I teach.
    5. I can state how a scripture has helped me personally.

    Class #4.6.

    Questions and Understanding

    1. I can use memorized questions to check for understanding.
    2. I can clarify difficult or unclear topics.
    3. I can ask appropriate questions to check for understanding.

    Class #4.7.

    Check Your Understanding

    1. I can ask simple and memorized who, what, when, where, why, and how questions.
    2. I can give appropriate feedback while listening to others (verbal and body language).
    3. I can restate what someone has answered to get clarification on my understanding.

    Class #4.10.

    My Mission Purpose

    1. I can greet and introduce myself to others.
    2. I can share a simple gospel message relevant to someone I meet.
    3. I can share a testimony tailored to a person I meet.
    4. I can use scriptures and other resources in a lesson.
    5. I can ask simple questions and respond appropriately to others' comments.
    6. I can extend an invitation and promise blessings.

    End of curriculum
